使用AntSword-Loader 启动antSword
<!--more-->


```bash
wget https://github.com/AntSwordProject/AntSword-Loader/releases/download/4.0.3/AntSword-Loader-v4.0.3-linux-x64.zip
unzip AntSword-Loader-v4.0.3-linux-x64.zip

wget https://github.com/AntSwordProject/antSword/archive/refs/tags/2.1.15.tar.gz
tar zxvf 2.1.15.tar.gz

mv antSword-2.1.15/node_modules/mime/mime.js antSword-2.1.15/node_modules/mime/Mime.js

```
